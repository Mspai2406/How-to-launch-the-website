How to launch your Website!
Do you want to launch website for free!!

Launching a website is what you need to do after you've designed and built a website (on your computer), and before you tell the world about your internet-accomplishments. This can be a tricky process if you're not very comfortable with how websites are placed and hosted on the internet, so take care to learn the right way to do it the first time.

Here is a complete guide to launch your website
Steps:

Select and register a domain name Choose a domain name that is brief, easy to remember, and suited to the content of your website. To get your domain for free, purchase the .cf domain. To get the domain click here.
Now you are directed to "freenom" website. Now type your domain name with .cf extentension. Click on check Availability.

If the given name is available, then click on checkout. If not available then check with other name.

The domain is free for only one year. After 12 months buy the same domain. So select it for 12 months.

Then click on Continue

Create an account then a verfication mail will be sent to your mail. Click on the link sent to your mail. After clicking on the given link your order will be confirmed.
Now click on click here to go to your client area or click here and then login to freenom.

In client area click on services -> My Domains -> Manage domain

Now click on Management Tools -> Namservers ,then click on use custom nameservers
Set the nameservers as ns1.byet.org, ns2.byet.org, ns3.byet.org...so on.
Click on Change nameservers.
